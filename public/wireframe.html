Our Philosophy
"To build in the tropics is to create a seamless transition between the shelter and the landscape."
We move away from enclosed, heavily air-conditioned concrete boxes. Our homes feature deep overhangs, internal courtyards, and natural material palettes that age beautifully over time.
Passive Cooling
Strategically placed courtyards and louvered windows channel natural breezes, dramatically reducing the need for mechanical cooling.
Natural Light Integration
Large sliding glass doors and skylights ensure every room is bathed in diffused, glare-free tropical sunlight throughout the day.
Turnkey Build Quality
From foundation to custom teak joinery, our in-house construction teams execute architectural designs with uncompromised precision.

Our Story
Pioneers of
Tropical Modernism.
For over two decades, we have been reshaping the Sri Lankan residential landscape by blending indigenous architectural wisdom with precise structural engineering.
Full-Width Hero Image · Architectural Sketch & Model · 21:9 ratio
Rooted in Context
Every piece of land has its own voice. Before drawing a single line, our architects spend hours on-site, understanding the wind patterns, the trajectory of the sun, and the existing foliage.
We don't impose generic concrete structures onto a plot. We sculpt spaces that invite the landscape inside, relying on natural materials like Kumbuk, Titanium poured concrete, and locally fired Terracotta.

Notes for Client

These wireframes represent the layout and content structure of each page. Final colours, typography, and imagery are already implemented in the live website.

Navigation
Fixed top bar on all pages. Contact strip displayed on homepage hero only. Mobile: full-screen slide-in menu.
Responsiveness
All grids collapse to single column on mobile. Service rows stack vertically. Navbar collapses to hamburger menu.
Image Placeholders
Cross-hatched boxes represent images. Final site uses high-quality Unsplash photography matching each section theme.
Interactions
Smooth scroll animations, hover effects on cards and nav links, hero image parallax, and mobile swipe-slide menus.
