The Kandy Retreat

Nestled in the hills, this project embodies our core philosophy. We utilized extensive overhangs and cross-ventilation strategies derived from traditional Sri Lankan architecture, interpreted through a modern, minimalist lens. The use of local Kumbuk wood and exposed fair-faced concrete creates a tactile dialogue with the surrounding jungle.

Our Heritage

Rooted in Sri Lankan Soil. Designed for Tomorrow.

At Modern House Construction, we don't just build walls; we craft lifestyles. Founded in 2008, our mission has always been to bridge the gap between luxurious modern aesthetics and the climatic realities of living in the tropics.

We draw inspiration from pioneers like Geoffrey Bawa, utilizing courtyards, natural light, and raw materials—concrete, timber, and terracotta—to create homes that age gracefully and require minimal maintenance.
